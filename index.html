    Cooking!
  1. coarsely chop and blanch garlic scapes in boiling water for 3 minutes
  2. shock blanched scapes in ice water until cooled through
  3. add 1/3C cashews, 1 packed cup of the blanched garlic scapes, 1/2 cup of grated parmesan, 1/2 tsp salt, and a few grinds of pepper to a blender on medium low speed
  4. slowly drizzle 1/4C olive oil while blending until the mix turns into a paste
  5. add to cooked pasta with 1/2C or so of cooked pasta water, top a sandwich, or freeze in an ice cube tray for later use! You can substitute other greens and nuts as needed. (if using another green, add as many cloves of garlic to the blender step as your heart tells you to)
